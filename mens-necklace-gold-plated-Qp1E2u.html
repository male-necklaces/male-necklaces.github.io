The 925 Sterling Silver Difference: Composition for Quality

What makes a sterling silver necklace men truly value? It's the "925" mark. This hallmark indicates the metal is 92.5% pure silver and 7.5% other metals, typically copper. Pure silver is inherently soft and prone to damage, so this specific alloy provides the necessary strength and hardness for durable jewelry.

This precise composition ensures your silver chain types for men can withstand daily use without easily deforming or scratching. The copper content in the alloy significantly enhances the silver's structural integrity, making it a robust choice.
