Karat Clarity: What Karat Gold is Best for Men's Chains?

The purity of gold in your male chain jewelry is measured in karats (k). This system tells you how much pure gold is present in an alloy, directly affecting its color, strength, and price point. Navigating these options helps you select a men's link necklace that perfectly suits your lifestyle and aesthetic preferences.

24 Karat gold contains 99.9% pure gold, while 18 Karat contains 75% pure gold, and 14 Karat contains 58.3%.

At the pinnacle of gold purity is 24 Karat gold, meaning it's almost entirely pure gold. While stunningly vibrant, its softness makes it impractical for everyday wear in a necklace mens chain. For this reason, gold is often alloyed with other metals like copper, silver, or zinc to enhance its strength and durability. 18 Karat gold comprises 75% pure gold, offering a rich luster with improved resilience. 14 Karat gold, with 58.3% pure gold, is even more robust, making it a popular choice for active individuals.
